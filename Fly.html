Bamboo Fly Rods

The first split-bamboo fly rods were developed in the 1850s, revolutionizing fly fishing with their superior casting ability and sensitivity.

Fly Fishing Origins

Fly fishing dates back to ancient Rome, where the first recorded mention of artificial flies was made by Claudius Aelianus in 200 AD.
